More than coffee


Last week at UCL, students and staff bought 1968 hot drinks from the Engineering Cafe.

If % of these drinks were served in a reusable cup:

Together we would have saved £ 1

Landfill waste would have been reduced by cm3 2

UCL's carbon footprint would have been reduced by kg of CO2 2

This is equivalent to kilometers on a London bus 3

This scenario represents a single week, in a single cafe. In that same week across UCL, 7976 hot drinks were served. And so on...
